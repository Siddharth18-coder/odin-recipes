SANDWICH RECIPE!!!!





Description

    Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (white or whole wheat)
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, or your choice)
  • 1-2 tablespoons of butter (softened)

    Steps:-

  1. Heat a skillet or non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  3. Place one slice of bread, butter side down, onto the skillet.
  4. Place cheese slices on top of the bread in the skillet.
  5. Top with the second slice of bread, butter side up.
  6. Cook until the bottom is golden brown, about 2-3 minutes.
  7. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  8. Remove from skillet, let it cool slightly, slice, and serve hot.
